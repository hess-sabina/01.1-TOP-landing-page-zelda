Now the legendary hero must explore a vast and
dangerous land and regain his memories
before Hyrule is lost forever. Want to know more?

CHAMPION POWERS

Urbosa’s Fury from Vah Naboris, the Camel. Sends a blast wave out from Link, damaging anything within the bubble and stunning them with electricity.

Revali’s Gale from Vah Medoh, the Bird. Sends Link soaring into the air before deploying the paraglider.

Mipha’s Grace from Vah Ruta, the Elephant. Fully restores Link’s hearts and adds five additional hearts.

Daruk’s Protection from Vah Rudania, the Lizard. Completely protects Link from any attacks, including sending a guardian beam back at the target.
